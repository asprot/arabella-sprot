ABOUT

Arabella made her debut playing at Montreux Jazz Festival in 2007...

In 2012 Arabella was awarded an Entry scholarship to study for a Masters Degree in Jazz Performance at Birmingham Conservatoire...

A second album was released during a 5-year stint living in Berlin from 2014 to 2018...

Currently based in West Sussex in the UK, Arabella is busy writing new material and also running an educational programme called The Jazz Workshop.
